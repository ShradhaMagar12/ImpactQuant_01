About ImpactQuant

Disaster Impact Analysis for Stocks, Sectors, and Recovery Patterns

The app uses the provided optimized CSV dataset, computes richer derived fields, and layers data science, visualization, and machine learning into separate route-based experiences.

01

Data Enrichment

Year, disaster category, sector profiles, location views, impact score, resilience score, and recovery-day estimation.

02

Filtering Layer

The same filters apply across Dashboard, EDA, Model, and Future Prediction pages for consistent exploration.

03

EDA Layer

Distribution, mix, heatmap, bubble, and scatter plots reveal where volatility and resilience live.

04

ML Layer

Random Forest classifier predicts impact level, while the regressor predicts change percentage for new scenarios.

Project Objective

Measure how disasters affect stock prices, identify vulnerable sectors, highlight resilient industries, and provide future-facing scenario prediction.

Methodology

Use descriptive statistics, correlation analysis, outlier detection, sector-level scoring, and machine learning to move from raw events to usable insight.
